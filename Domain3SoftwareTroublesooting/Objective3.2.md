# 3.2 Mobile OS & Application Troubleshooting Quick Notes

## Application Fails to Launch

**Note:** Verify app compatibility, restart device, and reinstall the app if needed.
**Example:** A banking app won't open after an OS update because the app version is outdated.

---

## Application Fails to Close / Crashes

**Note:** Force-close the app, clear cache, or reinstall if crashes continue.
**Example:** A game freezes and closes unexpectedly every time it loads a level.

---

## Application Fails to Update

**Note:** Check internet connectivity, available storage, and app store account status.
**Example:** An app update remains stuck at 50% because the device has no free space.

---

## Application Fails to Install

**Note:** Verify OS compatibility, storage availability, and app permissions.
**Example:** An app cannot be installed because it requires Android 15 while the phone runs Android 12.

---

## Slow to Respond

**Note:** Close background apps, clear storage, and restart the device.
**Example:** Opening the camera app takes 10 seconds because RAM is heavily used.

---

## OS Fails to Update

**Note:** Ensure sufficient storage, battery charge, and a stable internet connection.
**Example:** An iPhone update fails because only 1 GB of free storage is available.

---

## Battery Life Issues

**Note:** Reduce screen brightness, disable unused features, and check battery health.
**Example:** Battery drains quickly because GPS and Bluetooth are enabled all day.

---

## Random Reboots

**Note:** Check for faulty apps, overheating, OS corruption, or battery issues.
**Example:** A phone restarts whenever a specific social media app is opened.

---

## Bluetooth Connectivity Issues

**Note:** Verify Bluetooth is enabled, paired correctly, and within range.
**Example:** Wireless earbuds disconnect because they are not properly paired.

---

## Wi-Fi Connectivity Issues

**Note:** Check Wi-Fi settings, signal strength, and router functionality.
**Example:** A phone cannot browse the internet because it has an incorrect Wi-Fi password.

---

## NFC Connectivity Issues

**Note:** Ensure NFC is enabled and the device is close enough to the reader.
**Example:** Mobile payment fails because NFC is turned off.

---

## Screen Does Not Autorotate

**Note:** Verify auto-rotate is enabled and check for sensor issues.
**Example:** A phone remains in portrait mode because rotation lock is enabled.
